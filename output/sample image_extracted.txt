Prime Minister Narendra Modi has appealed to citizens to avoid buying gold, skip non-essential foreign trips, and work from home for the next year.

Here are the key reasons behind this appeal:

* Saving Foreign Exchange: India imports massive amounts of gold and crude oil.
Foreign travel and gold purchases drain huge amounts of US dollars out of the country.

* Global Crisis Impact: Ongoing geopolitical tensions and conflicts in the Middle East have driven up international crude oil prices significantly.

* Strengthening the Economy: During times of war and global instability, citizens are asked to make this ‘sacrifice’ to ease pressure on India's foreign exchange reserves and keep the Indian Rupee stable.
